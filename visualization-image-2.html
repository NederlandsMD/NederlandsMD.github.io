Humidity


The striking thing about the analysis of latitude vs. humidity is that so many places across latitudes have humidity between 80 and 100%. It is striking that both cities in low latitudes (winter) and those in high latitudes (summer) frequently have very high humidity levels. More uncommon is for humidity to be under 40%, usually around the middle latitudes.
