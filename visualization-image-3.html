Cloudiness


It seems that varying levels of cloudiness occur at all latitudes, and that the measurements for cloudiness are imprecise, given that nearly all fall along the same parameters (i.e. 75%, 20%, 40%). It is worth nothing that between 0 and 20 degrees north latitude very few cities have 0% cloudcover.
